Burger

Description:

This burger is a classic American favorite, made with a juicy beef patty, fresh lettuce, ripe tomatoes, and melted cheese, all sandwiched between a soft bun.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup lettuce, shredded
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1 teaspoon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat the grill or stovetop pan to medium-high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into 4 patties, about 1/2 inch thick.
  3. Season the patties with salt and pepper on both sides.
  4. Place the patties on the grill or pan and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until done to your liking.
  5. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheese on each patty and cover with a lid to melt the cheese.
  6. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or pan for 1 minute.
  7. Assemble the burgers by placing the cooked patties on the bottom buns, adding lettuce, tomato, and any desired condiments.
  8. Top with the remaining buns and serve immediately.
