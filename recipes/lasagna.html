Lasagna

Description:

This lasagna is a classic Italian comfort food that's perfect for any occasion. It's made with layers of rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and tender pasta sheets.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 15 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and drain the excess fat.
  4. Add the marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, 1 cup of mozzarella cheese, and the parmesan cheese.
  6. Assemble the lasagna by layering the noodles, meat sauce, and cheese mixture in a baking dish.
  7. Top with the remaining mozzarella cheese.
  8. Bake for 30 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
